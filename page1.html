Doudian is the e-commerce module of Douyin app that focuses on Â interest e-commerce. The interest e-commerce is an

e-commerce model that helps users explore their potential needs and make use of them. Users are using the Doudian while using the Douyin. Users can browse the commodity recommendation and evaluation videos made by various merchants or online celebrities through their preferences, so as to understand the characteristics of the commodity. When users are interested in purchasing, they can purchase directly through the links in the video. Doudian recommends products that users may like through the big data generated by users when browsing Douyin, so as to better meet the needs of users.
